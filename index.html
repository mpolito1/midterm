Welcome to our 2025 Summer season.

Royal Ranch Pool Club is a privately owned facility located in Queens, New York. We have been proudly serving the residents of our community for over 50 years. We offer a family friendly environment for those hot summer days where you can rest, relax and spend time with friends alongside the pool. Please contact us today to inquire about single or family membership opportunities.

The pool club offers the following amenities:

  • Adult Pool sized 75 ft x 35 ft ranging in depth from 3 ft to 9 ft.
  • Baby Pool sized 20 feet by 20 ft ranging in depth from 1 ft to 2ft.
  • Separate changing areas for Men and Women.
  • Shaded deck area.
  • Basketball and Paddleball court.
  • Large, shaded playground area with swingset.
  • Diving Board.
  • Swim lessons available to members for a fee.
  • Outside food and beverages are allowed however no glass containers are allowed.
  • Local restaurants deliver to the pool for your convenience.
  • Chairs and Chaise loungers are supplied by the pool.
  • Lots of street parking.
  • Members are allowed to bring guests for a fee.

Operating Season
The Royal Ranch Pool Club operates from Memorial Day Weekend until Labor Day.
